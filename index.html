Starter opportunity

Turn duplicate cards into rarity-based mystery inventory

Use low-cost common and uncommon cards as the base, then create higher tiers for rares, reverse holos, and special extras with clear guarantees.

  • Buyers choose rarity level, not the element.
  • Beads make the pull feel visual and fun.
  • Clear tier guarantees avoid overpromising hits.

FAQ

Clear rules make the shop feel trustworthy

Are official Pokemon logos or guaranteed rare cards included?

No official logos are used in this mockup. Product copy should only promise what your kids can reliably pack.

What condition should cards be in?

Keep damaged cards out of paid scoops. Light-play cards can go in a clearly labeled budget bin.

Mystery scoops

Pokemon card mystery scoops

Poke Pulls turns sorted bulk cards into a simple mystery pull: customers choose a rarity tier, then each bead color reveals the element of one card in the order.

How the scoop works

Every scoop has a fixed card count. A 30-bead scoop includes 30 cards. Beads do not add bonus cards; they map one-for-one to card elements.

Why rarity tiers matter

The tier sets the value expectation. Common, Rare, Premium, and Party Pack scoops each have their own card count and rarity guarantee.

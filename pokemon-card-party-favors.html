Why pack tiers work

The bead system makes the reveal easy to understand: one bead equals one card, and the color decides the card element.

Common + Pack scoops are not a promise of high-value cards. They are a fun way to distribute collector bulk with clear expectations, no damaged cards, and a pack-style bonus.

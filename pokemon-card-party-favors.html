Party favors

Pokemon card party favors

Poke Pulls Party Packs are built for birthdays, siblings, classroom prizes, and group openings where each child gets a simple mystery card moment.

Why party packs work

The bead system makes the reveal easy to understand: one bead equals one card, and the color decides the card element.

Honest expectations

Party packs are not a promise of high-value cards. They are a fun way to distribute collector bulk with clear rarity expectations and no damaged cards.

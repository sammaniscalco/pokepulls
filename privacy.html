Privacy

Privacy policy

This static MVP does not store payment data locally. Checkout and email signup data should be handled by configured providers.

Information collected

If checkout is enabled, purchase and payment details are handled by Stripe Checkout or another configured payment provider. If email signup is enabled, email addresses are sent to the configured signup provider.

Analytics

Analytics hooks are optional and disabled until a provider is configured in commerce-config.js.

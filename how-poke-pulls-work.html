How it works

How Poke Pulls work

Poke Pulls uses a simple bead reveal system. Customers choose the rarity tier, then each bead pulled becomes one card from the matching element bin.

1. Choose a rarity tier

The tier controls the card count and upgrade guarantee. Common, Common + Holo, Common + EX, and Common + Pack options are listed on the shop page.

2. Scoop the beads

Each bead color maps to an element such as Fire, Water, Grass, Lightning, Psychic, Fighting, Darkness, or Metal.

3. Pack one card per bead

A 50-bead Common + Pack scoop includes 50 cards. The packer selects one matching card for each bead, then adds the pack bonus promised by the tier.

FAQ

Can customers choose the element?

No. Customers choose only the tier. The bead scoop determines the element mix.

Are specific cards guaranteed?

No. No specific card, character, set, artwork, or market value is guaranteed.
